About Me

I am a full stack developer with a passion for problem solving and learning.

A lifelong love of technology has led me to development. As a child, I spent hours playing on computers, making powerpoints and LiveJournal layouts. As an Apple Genius, I provided thousands of customers with hardware and software solutions.

As an IT Service Engineer with SSI Schaefer, I supported large-scale automated warehouse systems software for companies like Wal-Mart and Kohls, ensuring that hundreds of thousands of customer orders were shipped on time.

These days, my focus is on web and software development. I love learning the intricacies of different technologies.

My free time is spent in the outdoors. I lead other kayakers on whitewater adventure trips and scale rock climbing walls. Sometimes mixing technology and nature can lead to some amazing results.
